Nagendra CM

CEO, Founder

A veteran of the semiconductor and power-electronics industry, Nagendra brings over three decades of global experience in component sales, strategic operations, and high-reliability systems. Having held leadership positions at Avnet, GE, ABB, and OmniOn Power (Acbel), he has been instrumental in driving innovation, acquisitions, and market growth across India’s technology ecosystem.

Renowned for his deep technical insight and commercial sharpness, he has led transformative initiatives spanning 5G, electric vehicles, data centers, defense, and industrial applications. His leadership style fuses engineering precision with business foresight—building teams that deliver scalable, high-performance solutions for mission-critical environments.

An alumnus of RV College of Engineering (RVCE) and Vidya Vardhaka Sangha, Nagendra also holds an MBA in Marketing. This blend of technical and business education empowers him to align cutting-edge innovation with customer-centric execution and market expansion strategies.

Shankar

CTO, Founder

In an industry where innovation, reliability, and deep technical insight define success, few professionals stand out the way Shankar M R does. With 35 years of hands‑on expertise in power supply design, he is widely regarded as one of the most seasoned and respected engineers in the field.

His mentorship has shaped countless engineers, and his contributions have strengthened the power electronics ecosystem in India and beyond. With 35 years of innovation behind him, Shankar continues to inspire the next generation of power supply designers. His work remains a benchmark for quality, reliability, and engineering excellence.
